WHO ARE WE

Trainersvault is a fitness company with a technology soul. Powered by a transformative business model proprietary technology, a deep understanding of culture, and some smartest people on earth, were in a position to challenge old systems and rewrite new rules. By giving personal trainers the same infrastructure as a 5,000 person fitness corporation, we’re changing the meaning of personal training - and revolutionizing fitness in the process.

PERSONAL TRAINERS ARE THE NEW GYM CORPORATIONS

WHAT WE BELIEVE

WE’RE NOT DISRUPTING AN EXISTING MARKET. WE’RE CREATING A NEW ONE.

While technology has evolved the fitness industry into the 21st century (on-demand training services, subscription based fitness videos, all you can access fitness studios) the personal trainer has been left behind.

By rethinking it, we’re championing a new model and reinventing fitness to be more personal and more in line with what actually generates results.

We exist because we believe the fitness industry should provide better opportunities for trainers and better options for consumers.

THIS IS HOW 21ST CENTURY FITNESS SHOULD WORK.

OUR MODEL

Empowering millions of consumers to have access to personal trainers around the world requires a unique infrastructure and the collaboration of many interdisciplinary teams. We understand this and we recognize that the soul of fitness is culture. We won’t just stop with technology we’re positioning our business to own the culture.

WER’RE NOT IN THE FITNESS BUSINESS. IN THE FITNESS, TECHNOLOGY, ENGINEERING-MEDIA PRODUCTION - TALENT MANAGMENT - CONSUMER ANALYTICS BUSINESS

  • PARTNERING

    We’re constantly welcoming new personal trainers to our platform to expand our ever - growing roster. To give consumers access to a broad range of training services, our team works directly with trainers helping them to list their 1-1 in person training availability, class schedules and online video training so that they are able train customers near and afar.

  • ENGINEERING

    To help our trainers improve monetization and customer experience, we build tools that serves as the backbone of their businesses. Engineering products like “Trainersvault Digital” allows trainers to quickly publish a series of videos and immediately sell and distribute them to consumers around the world.

  • MEDIA PRODUCTION

    Our media production team works with trainers all around the world bringing Hollywood to their community and customers. We make sure that we create quality content so that consumers are able to consistently receive the best content in a format that is professional and authentic to trainers’ brands.

  • TALENT MANAGEMENT

    Our Talent Management division work with fitness influencers across various demographics to build their brands and secure endorsements. Through partnerships and long standing relationships we are building an ecosystem where brands, content and consumers work together to create sustainable businesses for trainers.

  • LEARNING

    With every click, signup, class session booked or download, we gather an insane amount of data, informing every decision our company makes. Through the power of analytics we’re perpetually tweaking our model to make everything we do faster, smarter, easier and nimbler for trainers.
